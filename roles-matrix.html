Open questions before this can ship

  1. District Staff usage. No Alongside equivalent. Is TC using this heavily, or can we collapse to 4 roles? Affects whether we end at 4 roles + Clinical Staff (5) or 3 + Clinical Staff (4).
  2. "Other" in Alongside. What does it actually represent — office staff, coach, para, district observer? If anything outside School Staff, may need its own attribute.
  3. Counselor vs Social Worker vs Psychologist permissions. Are they functionally identical in practice, or does one have signoff authority another doesn't? Determines whether one Clinical Staff role is right.
  4. PHI default for school admins. Today Alongside Principal/Asst Principal often see student names. Should Merged School Admin default PHI = ON or OFF? Defaults shape onboarding friction.
  5. Crisis SMS sender cost. Defaulting Clinical Staff to receive Crisis SMS multiplies recipients — confirm with TC ops + Alongside on per-school SMS budget.
