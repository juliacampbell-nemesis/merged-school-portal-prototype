Help

Is my Self-Care activity visible to my district?

No. Self-Care is a private space for you. Your moods, chats with Kiwi, goals, and journal entries are not visible to your school admin or district.

How is Kiwi different from a therapist?

Kiwi is an AI companion trained by mental health experts to offer support and skills. Kiwi is not a substitute for clinical care. For ongoing therapy, ask your benefits team about EAP resources.

Can I delete my chat history?

Yes — open Chat > History > Settings to clear individual conversations or your entire history.

Is Self-Care included in my school's plan?

If your school has merged Alongside + TimelyCare, Self-Care is included for all staff at no additional cost.
